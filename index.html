Safety Tips While Waiting for a Tow Truck

If you find yourself stranded, follow these safety tips while waiting for us:

  • Stay Visible: Turn on your hazard lights to alert other drivers.
  • Remain Inside Your Vehicle: If you’re in a safe location, stay inside to avoid roadside dangers.
  • Move to a Safe Spot: Pull over to the shoulder or a nearby parking area if possible.
  • Prepare Your Documents: Have your driver’s license, insurance, and vehicle details ready.
  • Be Cautious of Strangers: Only interact with the official towing service personnel.
